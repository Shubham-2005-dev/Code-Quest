Code Quest

2025

CodeQuest 2025 is an exciting 48-hour hackathon where developers, designers, and innovators from all over the world come together to solve real-world challenges using cutting-edge technology. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a first-time hacker, CodeQuest provides a platform for creativity, collaboration, and building projects that can make an impact. Teams will work on solving problems in Al, web development, sustainability, and more, with opportunities to network, learn, and win amazing prizes.

Rules and Eligibility

Eligibility Criteria:

  • The hackathon is open to participants of all skill levels (beginner, intermediate, and expert).
  • Participants must be 18 years or older.
  • Teams can consist of 1 to 4 members.
  • Each participant must register individually.

Project Requirements:

  • All projects must be built from scratch during the hackathon.
  • The project must be related to the theme announced during the opening ceremony.
  • Teams must submit their final project before the submission deadline.

Code of Conduct:

  • Respect: All participants must show respect to others.
  • Collaboration: Collaboration is encouraged but plagiarism is not allowed.
  • No offensive content: Projects must not contain inappropriate content.
